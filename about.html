my personal story

I am a Master’s student in Informatics at Universitas Amikom Yogyakarta, where I was awarded a full scholarship. My research focuses on Natural Language Processing for mental health, particularly in multiclass and multimodal settings using transformer-based approaches.

My journey in AI began during my undergraduate studies and has since evolved into a deeper academic pursuit. I am particularly interested in how language models can be leveraged to understand complex human conditions and generate meaningful insights that go beyond conventional programming approaches.

Alongside my research, I actively build web-based implementations of AI systems using Flask and React, bridging the gap between theory and real-world applications. I also have professional experience as an Odoo developer, where I developed and customized modules based on client needs.

I am driven by the idea of creating intelligent systems that are not only technically sound but also impactful, especially in domains that directly relate to human well-being.
